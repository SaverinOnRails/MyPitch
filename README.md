# About
MyPitch is a free, open-source, simple, and customizable ear trainer for musicians. It helps train your ability to recognize the functions of scale notes within a tonal context and, by extension, your ability to recognize these pitches.
Grab the Windows and Android binaries from the release section, or try it in your browser now!
[MyPitch](https://www.saverinonrails.github.io)

MyPitch is still early in dev so bugs can be expected.

# Modes
There are four modes available in the app:

- Freeplay – Allows you to experiment with all the scale degrees, with an optional drone in the background to help you familiarize yourself with their sound and feeling.
- Interactive – Functions like a quiz. A scale degree is played, and your objective is to select the correct one.
- Pocketmode – For hands-free training. A note is played, and shortly after, an automatic human voice announces the correct scale degree, allowing you to train passively even when away from the program.
- Free Listen – The computer goes through the notes and plays them in random order.

In every mode, you can choose any tonic and octave, or have them selected randomly each round. You can also limit the scale degrees to a subset, allowing you to focus on areas that need improvement.
